About The Author
Hello, I am Joe D'Angelo.

Every time I look at the suffering, sorrow overwhelms me. I feel as if the world- the rich- is corrupt, just damaging those who are homeless. My purpose? To help those whom suffer in this way. It's wrong for them to suffer. They are human.

I would like you to consider looking at the kindness in your heart; to raise awareness not only within yourself, but within the world.

I've met with homeless people, people who can't afford to put food on their and their family's plates. I provide for them. And while they show appreciation, they have been corrupt as well, by those who hate. I strive to care for them, to cure, to support, to aid. Please join me on this journey of raising awareness throughout the world. It will warm your spirit, warm your soul and cure you of your sadness. Seeing a smile on someone else's face because of me makes my day, and it would to you too.
